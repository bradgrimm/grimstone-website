Grimstone • Riverton, Utah

Band Bio

Short Version (Social Media)

Grimstone is a high-energy punk/alt-rock cover band from Riverton, Utah. Specializing in 90s and 2000s rock anthems with an attitude-driven edge, we bring the sound of Blink 182, Foo Fighters, The Killers, and Jimmy Eat World to life with a few creative twists thrown in.

Medium Version (EPK Main Bio)

Grimstone is a Riverton, Utah-based rock band delivering high-octane performances of punk, alt-rock, and 90s/2000s favorites. Drawing from the era's defining acts (Blink 182, Green Day, Foo Fighters, The Killers, Weezer, Jimmy Eat World), the band combines tight musicianship with raw energy and attitude. Whether staying faithful to the originals or putting their own spin on classics, Grimstone brings an authentic rock experience to every show. With plans to begin writing original music in 2026, Grimstone is a band to watch in Utah's rock scene.

Long Version (Full Press Kit)

Grimstone is a five-piece rock band from Riverton, Utah, specializing in the punk, pop-punk, and alternative rock sounds that defined the 90s and 2000s. The band's setlist reads like a greatest-hits collection from the era: Blink 182, Green Day, Foo Fighters, The Killers, Weezer, Jimmy Eat World, The Strokes, and more.

Formed by experienced musicians with a shared love for high-energy, guitar-driven rock, Grimstone delivers performances that balance technical precision with the raw attitude these songs demand. The band takes a flexible approach to their covers, some played close to the original, others reimagined with creative arrangements and genre-bending twists.

Whether headlining a local venue, energizing a private event, or opening for touring acts, Grimstone brings an authentic rock experience built on solid musicianship and undeniable stage presence. As the band expands their repertoire and begins writing original material in 2026, they're positioned to become a fixture in Utah's rock scene and beyond.
