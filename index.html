About the Band

Grimstone brings high-energy punk and alternative rock to the stage, channeling the spirit of 90s and 2000s rock. Our mission is simple: play live, write originals, and keep the music fun and authentic.

Drawing inspiration from Jimmy Eat World, Pixies, Ramones, Foo Fighters, The Strokes, The Killers, and Franz Ferdinand, we blend punk attitude with pop sensibility. Whether we're covering classics or crafting our own sound, we bring raw energy and tight musicianship to every show.

We're all about nailing the core songs, adding some twisty genre-bent covers, and building a setlist that keeps the crowd moving. 2026 is the year we take our originals to the next level.
