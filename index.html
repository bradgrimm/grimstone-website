About the Band

Grimstone is a five-piece rock band from Riverton, Utah, built to keep a room moving. We play tight, high-energy sets of the anthems crowds already know — spanning 90s, 2000s, and classic rock — and we play them like we mean it.

From Blink-182 and The Killers to Journey, Van Halen, and KISS, our setlist is built to fill a dance floor and hold a room all night. We bring raw energy and tight musicianship to every show.

Alongside the covers, we perform our own original songs — Little Mouse, Are You Listening, and more — giving every set a sound that's unmistakably ours. Bar, brewery, private event, or festival, Grimstone brings the party.
